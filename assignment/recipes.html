Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix butter, sugar, and brown sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Mix all ingredients together.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Place spoonfuls of dough on a baking tray.
  8. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Allow cookies to cool before serving.
